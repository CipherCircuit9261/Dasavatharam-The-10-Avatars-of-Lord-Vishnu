He emerges as the divine embodiment of Vishnu's most potent weapon, the Sudarshana Chakra, signifying the guide to enlightenment. Found in dedicated shrines across Vishnu-associated temples, Chakrathazhwar is believed to embody the combined grace of Varaha and Narasimha.
